MISSION
KEEP THE CORRIDOR OPEN
Protect the carrier, escort the tankers through the Strait of Hormuz, and survive all six waves. Missiles, drones, and swarm boats will try to break the convoy. If the carrier is destroyed or every tanker is lost, the mission fails.

DEFEND THE STRAIT
How to play:
Click or tap anywhere to fire an interceptor from the carrier.
Destroy incoming missiles, drones, and swarm boats before they hit the carrier or your tankers.

Abilities:
1 = EMP Burst  ·  2 = CIWS Overdrive  ·  3 = Airstrike

You lose if:
the carrier is destroyed or all tankers are lost.

You win if:
you survive all 6 waves with at least one tanker alive.
